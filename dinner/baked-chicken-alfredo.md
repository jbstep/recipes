### Ingredients
 - ½ cup butter 
 - 1 pint heavy whipping cream (2 cups) 
 - 4 ounces cream cheese 
 - ½ teaspoon minced garlic 
 - 1 teaspoon garlic powder 
 - 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 
 - ¼ teaspoon salt 
 - ¼ teaspoon pepper 
 - 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
